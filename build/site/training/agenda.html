Top Gun Enablement Content Index and Agenda.

1. Tips, Recommendations.

  • Each of the links will take you to a new section. For a better experience, use the Open in a new tab browser option so you always have the agenda available.

  • Don’t Hurry! read carefully through the materials.

  • Understand and double check the TIPs/Notices/Warning Boxes, as they have essential information to complete the labs successfully

  • Don’t copy and paste through the exercises. Take your time to understand what you are doing

  • Most of the sections have additional docs. Check them out if time permits

  • The hands-on labs cover the basics of each feature. Think like a customer and try to go further and explore.

  • Most of the sections have a slide deck available, Check them out.
